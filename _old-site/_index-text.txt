The Experience
SimRace
1
Experience the thrill...
If you enjoy Karting, Track Days, Motorsports, Sim' Racing or just love Cars full stop, then you'll very likely love our race   experiences we offer too.
​
At our Swansea based venue we run a wide range of racing experiences for racers of all levels of abilities. If you're new to   Simulation Racing no problem - we're always on hand to guide
and assist you.
Motorsport Simulation, not an arcade: We have 8 interconnected state of the art Racing Simulators, race on your own or as a group.
​
Each of our Racing Simulators are equipped with:
Uber Wide Triple Screens which give you an 1.8 metre extra wide view of your surroundings.
Fanatec Direct Drive Steering Wheels which will give you a realistic race feeling experience.
Fanatec 100kg Load Cell Pedals - assured braking.
Sparco Race Seats which RUMBLE to the FEEL of the kerbs and to the bumps of the road surface.
All Cars and Drivers have the same wear and weight, unlike Karting there are no excuses...
​
Get to race on some of the most iconic tracks on the planet in Formula Cars, GT3 Cars, Le Mans LMPs and Touring Cars.
Socks, socks, socks! We recommend you to turn up in clean socks and whip your shoes off - otherwise turn up in flat soled trainers, shoes or even karting boots.  High heels? Nah...
​
At our teeny weeny venue we can cater to single racers and up to 8 racers in the same race.  Groups, birthday parties and corporate team building we can cater you.  We use Grown Up equipment here
