S1 // The Experience

Motorsport Simulation, Not an Arcade

If you enjoy karting, track days, motorsport or just love cars full stop, you'll love what we do. At our Swansea venue we run racing experiences for all abilities — and if you're new to sim racing, no problem: we're always on hand to guide and assist you.

Race on your own against the AI, or head-to-head with up to 8 racers in the same race. All cars and drivers run the same wear and weight — unlike karting, there are no excuses.

  • ✓Race iconic circuits — Spa, Monza, Silverstone, Le Mans, the Nordschleife and more.
  • ✓Drive real racing machinery — Formula cars, GT3, LMP & hypercars, touring cars.
  • ✓Beginner friendly — full guidance and a safety introduction before every session.

S2 // The Hardware

Pro-Grade Equipment,
Every Rig

Each of our eight simulators is built to give you a genuinely realistic race feel — the same gear used by esports pros and real-world drivers in training.

SPEC 01

Triple Screens

Uber-wide triple screens give you a 1.8 metre extra-wide view of your surroundings — see right into the apex.

SPEC 02

Fanatec Direct Drive

Direct drive steering wheels deliver realistic force feedback — feel the grip, the kerbs and every slide.

SPEC 03

100kg Load Cell Pedals

Fanatec load cell braking measures pressure, not travel — brake like you would in a real race car.

SPEC 04

Rumbling Race Seats

Sparco race seats rumble to the feel of the kerbs and the bumps of the road surface beneath you.

S5 // Before You Race

Restrictions & Good-to-Knows

We use grown-up equipment at SimRace1, so a few limits are set by the gear itself. Please check these before booking:

  • ✓Minimum age 13 — all racers must be 13 years or older.
  • ✓Minimum height 5ft — you need to reach the pedals properly.
  • ✓Maximum weight 20st / 130kg — set by the equipment we use.
  • ✓Up to 48″ waist — race seats are tight, just like the real thing.
  • ✓Safety briefing — all racers must be able to listen to our safety introduction.
  • ✓No spectating — wait-to-race seats only inside the venue.

Socks, socks, socks! Turn up in clean socks and whip your shoes off — or wear flat-soled trainers or karting boots. High heels? Nah…

Additional, special or physical needs? Contact us before booking and we'll do our best to help.
